Private Data I Receive And Collect

Nothing personal. Why? I don't need such information to let Helia, my bot, to function. Only data needed for functionality is stored, nothing more. Nobody other than the staff maintaining the bot will have access to any data. I pledge to never share any details of any kind with external persons or organizations. Not for legal, not for monetary, not for any reason, ever.

What I do collect

  • Discord User ID's
  • Discord User names
  • Discord Server ID's
  • Discord Channel ID's
  • Discord Role ID's
  • Any other information that is essential to Helia bot

How I Use This Data

Helia uses it's users' data for the following purposes:

  1. To identify who executed a command
  2. To ensure command are executed properly
  3. To avoid confusion / impersonation / security risks

In the first period of the time Helia had different names (a lot), just a few commands, It was just some experimenting. After around 8 months, I decided to try to take it to 75 Servers. Of course I hate advertizing just as you do. But just standing here, doing nothing won't get me any closer to my goals.
